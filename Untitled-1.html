Welcome to Bravin's World

Welcome to the Digital Universe!

Bravin was not like other kids. While they were outside playing football, he was inside, dismantling old computers to see how they worked...

From the age of five, he was captivated by the glow of the screen and the magic of pressing keys. He didn’t just use computers—he wanted to understand them. As he grew older, he collected discarded computer parts, visiting repair shops and scavenging old motherboards, CPUs, and RAM sticks. By ten, he had built his first working computer entirely from scraps, a feat that stunned his family and friends. But building computers wasn't enough. He wanted to explore the digital world and learn how websites functioned. At thirteen, he dove into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, spending countless hours experimenting and coding simple programs. His dream was to create something that others could see and interact with. At fifteen, after months of relentless effort, he finally launched his first website—a digital space where he shared his thoughts, inventions, and discoveries. His journey was only beginning, and he knew this was just the start of something much greater.
